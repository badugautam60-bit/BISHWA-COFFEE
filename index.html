Our Story

A street cafe with a soul

Bishwa Coffee started as a simple idea — give Thamel a proper neighbourhood cafe. Not a tourist trap, not a chain. Just honest coffee, a worn wooden chair, and the kind of place you come back to every morning.

Tucked just steps from the Garden of Dreams, we're where locals start their day and travellers find their footing. Every cup is brewed with intention, every seat is yours for as long as you need it.
